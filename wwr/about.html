Our company, the Whitewater Rafting Company, has a proud history of serving in the local community. We have been operating since 1999, when our founder, Titus B. Simmons, dedicated his time to serving as a river guide. During Titus's venture as a guide, he discovered to growing need for a rafting company and created this company with a few other local river guides. The Whitewater Rafting Company began small, and now hosts multiple tours and trips weekly in the season.
